About My Capstone Project

Looking back at my experiences as a technical writing and communication major, I've had the opportunity to flex my writing muscles in multiple genres and media. This portfolio exemplifies some of my favorite projects and revised works along with a reflection of my experience in the technical writing and communication program.

Metro Transit Blue Line Light Rail (BLRT) Extension Magazine Article

WRIT 3671 - Visual Rhetoric and Document Design: This Metro Transit BLRT magazine article was part of my final digital humanities project in which I was assigned to create an original representation of information and data through visual and rhetorical decisions.

Female Athletes Don't Throw Like Girls

WRIT 3244W - How Words Change the World: A look at the discourse of being a female in athletics and how language shapes the environment.

Dakota Access Pipeline DAPL Environmental Report

WRIT 3102W - Public Writing: The final project for this course was to pick an environmental issue and research the impact, alternatives and possible solutions which then would be compiled in an all encompassing document.
